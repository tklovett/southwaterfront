Who We Are

Welcome to Southwaterfront.org, a community organization of residents and businesses in the South Waterfront neighborhood of Portland, OR. This portal was created for the community to better come together and advocate for issues and events that are important to us. Different from a traditional informational website, or community association, this portal is specific to identifying problems, and working with our local government and property owners to develop solutions.

The Issue

One of the issues that we are currently trying to help you resolve is the need for a grocery store in South Waterfront. A grocery store is a keystone of a livable, walkable, urban community, yet despite significant investment, density, and demand, South Waterfront has been unable to attract a grocer. Part of this is that the neighborhood population is just getting to the point to support a grocer, but perhaps part of it is that the community does not have a tool to effectively communicate their desire to attract a grocer to the neighborhood. We would like to help you to communicate directly to the stores to promote your community as the right place for the store.
